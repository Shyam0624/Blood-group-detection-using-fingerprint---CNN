About Us

The Bloodgroup Detector is a cutting-edge tool that allows users to detect their blood group using fingerprint technology. Our mission is to simplify and modernize the process of blood group detection by utilizing the latest advancements in image processing and artificial intelligence.

This system is designed to be user-friendly, accurate, and efficient. Whether you are at home or on the go, our platform can provide you with fast results without the need for traditional blood testing methods. With a simple scan of your fingerprint, you can easily determine your blood group within minutes.
